EDIFICE FUNCTIONAL DEFINITION
-----------------------------

This message is designed to facilitate the exchange information between a
Manufacturers Agent (commonly referred to as a Distributor, or Stockist) and
the Manufacturer to identify those of the manufacturer's products which have
been sold on to or credited to a third party customer during the specified time
period(s) for which the message is transmitted.

The minimum anticipated requirement is for the Agent to identify each product
resold, the resale unit price and the total quantity sold of that product.

Additional capability is included to facilitate transmission of end customer
(or third party) identification information, and the quantity of product sold
to that specified end customer.

The resale message has also been designed to facilitate automatic processing of
Credit claims from the Agent to the Manufacturer. Where specific invoice and
resale price information, together with such additional information as may be
required, is provided in the message in respect to any product and quantity
declared,the resale message provides the required information for the
manufacturer to process a credit claim.

The ship & debit business practise is common in the Electronics Industry where
the Agent (Distributor) will frequently hold a stock, purchased by the Agent at
a fixed price, with assumptions regarding expected margins (profit on the
resale).  For example, where the Agent sells at below his cost, or at a resale
price below that to obtain the expected margin, the Manufacturer may
give a dispensation to the Agent for that price and accept liability to support
the Agent's margin via a pre-approved quote at time of Invoicing by the Agent
to the end customer.

EXPLANATORY NOTES
-----------------

The following codes are used to indicate, in a more detailed and precise way
than EDIFACT, the usage of Segments and Data Elements in the EDIFICE
Guidelines:

                   EDIFACT                  EDIFICE

                   Mandatory                Mandatory
                   -                        -
                   Conditional              Required
                   -                        -
                   Conditional              Advised
                   -                        -
                   Conditional              Depending
                   -                        -
                   Conditional              Special Agreement
                   -                        -
                   Conditional              Not used
                   -                        -

Mandatory = EDIFACT dictates that the Data Element or Segment must be present

Required  = EDIFICE members agree that the Data Element or Segment must be
            present at least once

Advised   = EDIFICE advises that the Data Element or Segment should be used,
            this is a recommendation only

Depending = The presence of the the Data Element or Segment is conditioned.
            Dependency will be explained at the approriate level of detail

Special   = The Data Element or Segment will only be used if Agreement the two
            trading partners have specifically agreed to do so in a Trading
            Partner Agreement

Not Used  = The Data Element or Segment will not be used by EDIFICE members

Where a Composite Data Element is indicated as Not Used then, the column 'usage
status' for the Component Data Elements will remain blank.

The number of occurrences shown in the message diagram indicates the maximum
number of occurrences for the EDIFICE utilisation.

The EDIFICE useage status and number of occurrences for segments or segment
groups will be represented analog to the representation of data elements

eg.:  R3   The segment or -group is requried 3 times (fixed number)
      R..3 The segment or -group is requried up to 3 times (maximum number)
